title: Home DAP Agency - Issuers in the same DAP Agency

participant User
participant DA Issuer
participant DAP Agency
participant DLT
participant Credential Issuer
participant VC Store

User->DA Issuer: Flow 1:Request a DA
DA Issuer->DAP Agency: TA,DA Collision checks
DAP Agency->DA Issuer: Success - Create DA + @DAP_ID suffix
DAP Agency-->DLT: Save DA Metadata (DA,USER_DAP_ID,ISSUER_ID, DAP_AGENCY_ID)
DAP Agency-->DAP Agency: User Home DAP Established
DAP Agency->DA Issuer: DA Success
DA Issuer->User: Send DA (QR, Email, etc)
User->Credential Issuer: Flow 2: Request VC
Credential Issuer-> User: TA Collision + Link DA
Credential Issuer->DAP Agency: Resolve HOME_DAP_ID from DA
DAP Agency->DLT: Lookup DAP Suffix in DA
DLT->DAP Agency: Resolved DAP_AGENCY_ID, DAP_DA
DAP Agency->Credential Issuer: Resolved DAP_ID, DAP_DA
Credential Issuer->VC Store: Issue Credential
Credential Issuer-->DAP Agency: Save Credential Metadata (DA, USER_DAP_ID, ISSUER_ID)
DAP Agency--> DLT: Save VC Metadata (DA,USER_DAP_ID, ISSUER_ID, DAP_AGENCY_ID)
DAP Agency->User: VC Notification
