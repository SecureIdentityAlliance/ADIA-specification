title Issuer Initiated Account Deletion for unclaimed accounts

participant Issuer
participant User
Participant DAS
Participant ARD

Issuer->User: Accept the created Digital Address.
User->Issuer: Reject
Issuer-> DAS: Delete Unclaimed DA \n(DA, DAS_USER_ID,ISSUER_INITIATED=Yes)
DAS -> ARD: Delete DAS_USER_ID
ARD -> ARD: Lookup DAS_USER_ID and delete HIDA entry
ARD -> DAS: Delete success
DAS -> DAS: Lookup and delete DAS_USER_ID and delete any corresponding credentials
DAS-->Issuer: Deletion Success
